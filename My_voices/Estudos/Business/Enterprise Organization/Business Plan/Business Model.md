
# Definition

Document that will help you—and anyone you approach for funding—to understand what your business will do and how all its key parts fit together

# What it Must Have

## Market
### Revenue sources
The different branches of the business the money comes from (B2C and B2B, ads, services, etc.)

### Cost drivers
Fixed costs to run the company and variable costs that may vary with volume.

### Investment size
Measurable level of investment to get off the ground and keep it operating.

### Critical success factors
Might be the ability to roll out new products on a sustained basis, success in reaching some critical mass of business within a certain time, and so on.”

## Delivery 
### Discovery Plan

Plan to attract you target costumers, for this:
 1. Know your customers well enough to reach them
 2. To be competitive, be creative
	- Go beyond IG, Facebook, etc.
	- Go directly to the customers
