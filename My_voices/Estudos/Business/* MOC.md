# Business Plan

## Definition

A comprehensive roadmap outlining a company's objectives and the specific actions required to achieve them. It describes how various business branches interact to create a competitive advantage and deliver a product that provides value to both the enterprise and its customers.

---

## Core Pillars

- [[Business Model]]: Focuses on market and costumer delivery.
- [[Business Strategy]]: Focuses on product differentiation, competitive positioning, and resource allocation.

---
## Strategic Inquiries

> [!QUOTE]
> "Does the plan clearly articulate how the business will differentiate itself? Does it identify the resources required to execute the strategy? Is there a clear path to value creation for the target customer?"
