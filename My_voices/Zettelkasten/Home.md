# 🏠 Home

> [!INFO] How this vault works
> This is a **Zettelkasten** second brain. Notes are atomic and navigated by **links + Maps of Content (MOCs)**, not by the folder tree. Every note carries an `id`, a `type` (`fleeting` → `permanent` → `index`), and an `up:` link to its parent map.
> **New here? Read [[VAULT_SPECS]] first.**
